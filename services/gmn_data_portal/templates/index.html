About

The Global Meteor Network (GMN) is a worldwide organization of amateur and professional astronomers alike, whose goal is to observe the night sky using low-light video cameras and produce meteor trajectories in a coordinated manner. Here you will find information on how to access open access meteor data produced by the network.
